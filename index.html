About
HPV

There is a prevailing misconseption
among sexual minority people assigned
female at birth (AFAB) that they are
highly unlikely to get a sexually transmitted
disease (STD) from their
AFAB partners.

This is not the case!
Studies show that about 50% of
sexual minority individuals
AFAB have been infected with at least
one type of HPV.
